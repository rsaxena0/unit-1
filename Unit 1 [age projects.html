Unit 1: The Online World.

Introduction On this unit, pass, merit and distinction is determined from taking a set exam, unlike the other units where it is determined by coursework. This unit is gaining knowledge about the modern online world, online services, the range of the online services including: email, online data storage, collaborative software, search engines and blogging. Also in this unit we have to investigate the technology and software that support the online services.
